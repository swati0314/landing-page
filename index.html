About Khana Khazana Mallu Style



Welcome to Khana Khazana Mallu Style, where we bring you the authentic flavors of South Indian cuisine, with a special focus on the vibrant and delectable dishes from Kerala, often referred to as 'God's Own Country'.

Our skilled chefs bring years of experience and a passion for traditional cooking methods, ensuring that every bite you take is a journey through the rich culinary heritage of Kerala.

From spicy curries to fluffy appams and everything in between, we are dedicated to delivering an exceptional dining experience that captures the essence of Mallu style cooking.
